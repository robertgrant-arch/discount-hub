# DiscountHub — Design Ideas

## Approach A: "Civic Trust" — Government-Inspired Clarity
- **Design Movement**: American civic modernism meets fintech clarity
- **Core Principles**: Authoritative typography, high-contrast navy/gold palette, structured data tables, institutional trustworthiness
- **Color Philosophy**: Deep navy (#0A1628) with gold accents (#C9A84C) — evokes government seals, Medicare cards, official documents. Communicates reliability.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Left-rail category navigation with main content grid; sidebar always visible on desktop
- **Signature Elements**: Horizontal rule dividers with gold accent, badge-style discount tags, official-looking card borders
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Minimal animation, focus on legibility and accessibility (large text, high contrast)
- **Animation**: Subtle fade-ins only; no distracting motion
- **Typography System**: Merriweather (serif, headings) + Source Sans Pro (body) — institutional and readable
- **Probability**: 0.07

## Approach B: "Warm Abundance" — Lifestyle Magazine Energy
- **Design Movement**: Modern lifestyle editorial meets SaaS product
- **Core Principles**: Warm terracotta/cream palette, generous whitespace, editorial card layouts, human photography focus
- **Color Philosophy**: Terracotta (#C4622D), warm cream (#FDF6EC), forest green (#2D5016) — evokes warmth, abundance, active senior lifestyle. Approachable and optimistic.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric editorial grid with large feature cards and smaller supporting cards; masonry-style discount listings
- **Signature Elements**: Rounded pill badges for discount amounts, warm gradient overlays on cards, editorial-style section headers
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Hover lifts cards, smooth category transitions, satisfying micro-interactions
- **Animation**: Spring-physics card hovers, staggered list entrances, smooth tab transitions
- **Typography System**: Playfair Display (headings) + DM Sans (body) — editorial warmth with modern readability
- **Probability**: 0.09

## Approach C: "Sharp Savings" — Bold Fintech Challenger
- **Design Movement**: Challenger bank / neo-fintech boldness (think Robinhood, Monzo)
- **Core Principles**: High-contrast dark mode, electric teal/white accents, data-forward layout, premium membership feel
- **Color Philosophy**: Deep charcoal (#111827) background, electric teal (#0ECFCF) primary, pure white text — creates urgency and value perception. "Your money working harder."
- **Layout Paradigm**: Full-width dark hero, sticky top nav, horizontal scrolling category pills, card grid with glassmorphism effects
- **Signature Elements**: Glowing teal discount badges, frosted glass membership cards, animated savings counters
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Every interaction feels premium — hover glows, smooth reveals, satisfying click states
- **Animation**: Glow pulse on CTAs, slide-up card entrances, number counter animations for savings stats
- **Typography System**: Space Grotesk (headings — geometric, modern) + Inter (body) — tech-forward but readable
- **Probability**: 0.08

---

## Selected Approach: **B — "Warm Abundance"**

Chosen because it best serves the target audience (seniors and disabled Americans) with warmth, approachability, and editorial clarity. The terracotta/cream palette feels welcoming rather than clinical, while the editorial card layout makes browsing 280+ discounts feel curated rather than overwhelming. The lifestyle magazine energy communicates abundance and value — perfect for a membership product.
